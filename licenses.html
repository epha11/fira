Government Compliance and Authorizations

1. International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR)

Shadeware ensures strict adherence to ITAR regulations, which govern the export and import of defense-related articles and services. This certification confirms that our cyber tools and products align with U.S. national security and foreign policy goals. All employees involved with ITAR-regulated materials undergo thorough background checks and training.

2. National Industrial Security Program Operating Manual (NISPOM)

As a contractor handling classified information, Shadeware complies with NISPOM standards. This includes secure storage, access controls, and rigorous audits to safeguard sensitive government information from unauthorized access.

3. Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC)

We are certified at CMMC Level 5, the highest level of cybersecurity compliance. This reflects our robust defenses against advanced persistent threats, ensuring the protection of federal contract information and controlled unclassified information (CUI).

4. Export Administration Regulations (EAR)

Shadeware complies with EAR regulations to manage the export of dual-use items effectively. This ensures all technology with both civilian and military applications is properly controlled and lawfully distributed.

5. Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement (DFARS)

We implement stringent DFARS requirements to safeguard covered defense information (CDI) and ensure accurate cyber incident reporting as required for government contracts.
